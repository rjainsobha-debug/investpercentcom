Biggest Property Investment Mistakes
Avoid these costly errors before committing your capital.
01
Buying without ROI analysis
Most buyers never model ROI. Use this tool before signing any agreement — every deal should show >8% ROI.
02
Ignoring rental yield
Net yield below 3% means your property can't service its EMI from rent. Calculate net yield first.
03
Overleveraging EMI
EMI above 40% of income leaves no buffer for repairs, vacancies or life events. Keep it manageable.
04
Wrong location selection
Location drives 70% of return. Score infrastructure, connectivity and rental demand before buying.
