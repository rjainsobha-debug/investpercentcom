Travel Insurance — Don't Leave India Without It

A hospital bill in the USA can be $50,000 (₹42 lakh). Emergency evacuation from Europe: $30,000. Travel insurance for 2 weeks: ₹2,000–5,000. The math is obvious.

What Does Travel Insurance Cover?

🏥
Medical Emergency

Hospital bills, emergency surgery, doctor visits abroad. Most important coverage.

🚁
Medical Evacuation

Air ambulance to fly you home or to nearest suitable hospital. Can cost $30K+.

✈️
Trip Cancellation

Refund of non-refundable flights/hotels if you can't travel due to illness, death in family.

🧳
Baggage Loss

Compensation if airline permanently loses your checked baggage.

⏰
Flight Delay

Meals and accommodation costs if flight delayed 6+ hours.

🛂
Passport Loss

Covers emergency passport replacement costs abroad.

What's NOT covered: Pre-existing conditions (usually), adventure sports (add-on needed), alcohol-related incidents, self-harm, war zones. Read exclusions carefully.

How Much Coverage Do You Need?

  • USA/Canada: Minimum $100,000 medical coverage (healthcare is extremely expensive)
  • Europe (Schengen): Minimum €30,000 — legally required for Schengen visa
  • Southeast Asia: $50,000 is usually sufficient
  • Middle East: $50,000–$100,000
Real Cost Without Insurance

Traveller from India to USA suffers a heart attack. Hospitalisation: $80,000. Stent surgery: $40,000. ICU: $15,000. Medical evacuation: $25,000.
Total: $1,60,000 = ₹1.34 Crore
Travel insurance for 2 weeks USA trip: ₹3,500–6,000. Never skip it.

Best Travel Insurance Providers from India

  • Bajaj Allianz Travel Insurance: Most popular, wide coverage, strong claim support
  • HDFC Ergo Travel: Competitive premium, fast claim settlement
  • TATA AIG Travel Guard: Good for families, includes adventure sports add-ons
  • Care (formerly Religare) Travel: Good coverage-to-premium ratio
  • Digit Insurance: 100% online, simple policy, good for millennials
Travel credit card bonus: Some premium credit cards (Amex Platinum, HDFC Infinia) include complimentary travel insurance up to $200,000. Check your card benefits before buying separately.
