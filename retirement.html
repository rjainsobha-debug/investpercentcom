Why this section matters

This page is designed to stay inside the same premium family as Investment, Loan and Tax — not as a stripped-down template.

Retirement is a system problem

Users don’t just need one corpus number. They need to understand time, income, inflation and longevity together.

FIRE and traditional retirement can coexist

Some users want early independence, others want classic retirement security. Both belong in the same premium section.

Post-retirement cash flow matters most

A large corpus feels impressive, but what matters is whether it can support real monthly living over decades.
