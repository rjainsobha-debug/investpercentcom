FIRE — Financial Independence, Retire Early

FIRE is not about being lazy. It's about building enough wealth that work becomes optional. Calculate your FIRE number, choose your strategy, and build your path to freedom.

What is FIRE?

FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) is a movement built on one core idea: aggressively save and invest 40–70% of your income until your investments can fund your lifestyle indefinitely — without needing a job.

The goal isn't necessarily to stop working — it's to make work optional. Many FIRE adherents continue doing meaningful work because they choose to, not because they have to.

Your FIRE Number — The 25× Rule

Your FIRE number is 25 × your annual expenses (based on the 4% safe withdrawal rate). Once your investments reach this number, you can withdraw 4% annually and statistically never run out of money.

FIRE Number Calculation

Current monthly expenses: ₹75,000 → Annual: ₹9,00,000
FIRE Number: ₹9,00,000 × 25 = ₹2.25 Crore
At 12% CAGR, ₹30,000/month SIP hits ₹2.25 Cr in ~18 years from zero.
Increase savings rate → retire faster.

India-specific note: Use a 3–3.5% withdrawal rate (not 4%) due to India's higher inflation. This means multiplying annual expenses by 29–33×.

Types of FIRE

🔥 Lean FIRE

Minimal lifestyle. Annual expenses under ₹4L. Works if you live frugally and are happy with less. FIRE number: ₹1–1.5 Cr.

🏠 Fat FIRE

Comfortable lifestyle without compromise. Expenses ₹15–25L/year. FIRE number: ₹4–7 Cr. Requires high income.

⚡ Barista FIRE

Partially retire early. Do part-time, passion work that covers basic expenses. Portfolio covers the rest. Best of both worlds.

🌊 Coast FIRE

Invest aggressively early, then stop — let compounding do the rest. At 25, ₹30L invested at 12% grows to ₹1.2 Cr by 50 without adding more.

The FIRE Formula: Savings Rate = Speed

  • 10% savings rate: ~43 years to FIRE
  • 25% savings rate: ~32 years to FIRE
  • 40% savings rate: ~22 years to FIRE
  • 60% savings rate: ~12 years to FIRE
  • 75% savings rate: ~7 years to FIRE

Increasing income AND reducing expenses simultaneously is the fastest FIRE path. Focus on both sides of the equation.
