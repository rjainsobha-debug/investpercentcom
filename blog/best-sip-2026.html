Best SIP Plans for 2026 — Our Top Picks

With over 1,400 mutual funds available in India, choosing where to start your SIP is overwhelming. We've filtered the best across categories based on consistency, expense ratio, and risk-adjusted returns — not just 1-year hype.

How We Selected These Funds

We evaluated funds across five criteria: 5-year and 10-year CAGR vs benchmark, expense ratio (direct plan), fund manager tenure, consistency of outperformance (rolling returns), and AUM stability. We deliberately excluded funds with spectacular 1-year returns but inconsistent long-term track records.

All returns mentioned are for the Direct Plan. If you invest through a distributor or bank, switch to Direct immediately — the cost difference compounded over 20 years is significant.

What to Avoid in 2026

  • Thematic funds (PSU, Defence, Consumption): These are cyclical bets. Most underperform over 10-year cycles.
  • NFOs (New Fund Offers): "New" is not "better." No track record = pure speculation.
  • International funds: Regulatory changes + currency risk make these complex for most investors.
  • Funds with 3%+ expense ratios: These are regular plans with distributor commissions eating into your returns.
